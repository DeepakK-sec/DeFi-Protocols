# Major DeFi Protocol Categories

This folder contains detailed explanations of the main types of DeFi protocols. Each category gets its own file so things stay organized and easy to read.

Click on any link below to jump into that category:

- [Decentralized Exchanges (DEXs)](dex.md)  
  How you swap one token for another without a central company — Uniswap, Curve, and more.

- [Lending & Borrowing](lending.md)  
  Earn interest on your crypto or borrow against it — Aave, Compound, MakerDAO, etc.

- [Yield Farming & Liquidity Providing](yield.md)  
  How people earn extra rewards by helping protocols run smoothly.

- [Stablecoins](stablecoins.md)  
  Cryptocurrencies designed to stay steady at $1 (or another fixed value) — USDC, DAI, USDT, and newer ones.

- [Derivatives & Synthetics](derivatives.md)  
  Ways to bet on price movements or create synthetic assets — Synthetix, GMX, dYdX.

- [Insurance](insurance.md)  
  Protect yourself against hacks, bugs, or other risks — Nexus Mutual and others.

- [Emerging Trends](emerging.md)  
  New and exciting stuff like restaking (EigenLayer), real-world assets (RWAs), perpetuals, and more.

Each file starts with a simple explanation, then goes deeper into how the protocols work, popular examples, benefits, and risks.

Pick whichever interests you most — there’s no strict order!
